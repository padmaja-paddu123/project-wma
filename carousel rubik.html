RULES OF RUBIKS

Rubiks game rules

Objective: Solve the cube by rotating the layers to align the colors on each face.

Notation: - F (Front): Face in front of you - B (Back): Face opposite the front - U (Up): Top face - D (Down): Bottom face - L (Left): Left face - R (Right): Right face - x (rotate): Rotate the top layer (U) clockwise - x' (rotate counter-clockwise): Rotate the top layer (U) counter-clockwise

Basic Rules: 1. Start with a scrambled cube. 2. Solve the cube by rotating the layers. 3. Each face must have a solid color. 4. All corners and edges must be in their correct positions.

Solving Steps: 1. White Cross (Top Layer): Solve the white cross on the top layer. 2. White Corners (Top Layer): Solve the white corners. 3. Middle Layer: Solve the middle layer edges. 4. Yellow Cross (Bottom Layer): Solve the yellow cross on the bottom layer. 5. Yellow Corners (Bottom Layer): Solve the yellow corners. 6. Orientation: Orient the last layer colors. 7. Permutation: Permute the last layer pieces.

Competition Rules (Speedcubing): 1. Timer starts when the cube is scrambled. 2. Solver has 15 seconds to inspect the cube. 3. Solver must solve the cube within the time limit. 4. Fastest solve time wins.

Penalties: 1. DNF (Did Not Finish): Failure to solve the cube. 2. DNS (Did Not Start): Failure to begin solving.

Common Techniques: 1. Layer-by-Layer 2. Block Building 3. Edge Pairing 4. Corner Pairing 5. Orientation Last (OLL) 6. Permutation Last (PLL)

Popular Solving Methods: 1. Fridrich Method 2. Petrus Method 3. CFOP (Cross, F2L, OLL, PLL)

Rubik's Cube Variations: 1. 2x2 (Mini Cube) 2. 4x4 (Rubik's Revenge) 3. 5x5 (Professor's Cube) 4. Blindfolded Solving 5. One-Handed Solving Now, go ahead and cube on!
